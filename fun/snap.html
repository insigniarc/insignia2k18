#SNAPSHOT ROUND 2#
RULES: Snap must be based on any one out of the following concepts :-
1) A picture showing celebration of a prominent festival in their culture that is descriptive enough.
2) Capture an event that occurs too fast for the eye to perceive. Things like the bursting of a water-filled balloon, or a light-bulb smashing on concrete etc.
3) Candidate's picture savouring their favorite food.
#P.S - Picture can be solo or group.
For 2) concept , it is not necessary for the participant to be a part of the snap.
#Round 2 starts on April 1 and ends on April 5 at 10:00 a.m.
#Judgement will be done by the teachers and the pictures of the winners will be posted after announcing the results for round 2.
